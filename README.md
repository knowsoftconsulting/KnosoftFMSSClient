Knowsoft FMSS (Financial Management System Strengthening eTools) is a powerful offline desktop application designed for NGOs, UNICEF partners, and development organizations.
It features eFACE Form Generator, Electronic Fixed Asset Register with depreciation & charts, eProcurement with vendor scoring & LPO, and full Inventory Management system.
Generate professional PDF reports with your logo, track assets, manage vendors, and maintain inventory — all in one secure, user-friendly tool.
